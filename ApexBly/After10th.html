High school is an important stepping stone in the academic future of a student. The inquisitiveness of selecting a particular stream to study ahead is a task cut out for the students. Chandigarh University makes that easy for them by offering various diploma programs for the students once they pass out of high school. It includes engineering programs such as Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science Engineering etc. Chandigarh Polytechnic College functions ceaselessly with a global approach to attain industry demands by offering you lucrative semester exchange programs which enable you to go abroad and study there during the tenure of their course. This gives our students the cutting edge over others and helps in incubating a professional mark. Chandigarh University is the first university in the region which has tie ups with more than 200 foreign universities out of which more than 40 belong to less than 500 QS ranking category. CPC imparts long term technical and professional skills to the students to assist them in their overall development.

Why CPC?

CHANDIGARH POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE (CPC), a magnificent institute, under the aegis of Chandigarh Educational Trust, has achieved a unique position in serving Punjab state in the field of technical education by not only offering world class education but also providing cutting edge to technical education with attractive diploma placements, state of art infrastructure and highly experienced faculty. We, at Chandigarh Polytechnic College endeavor towards the identification of best talents and hone their skills to make them fulfill industry demands.


Bestowed with the “Outstanding Technical Institution Award" By NITTR, Chandigarh

Chandigarh Polytechnic College received "Outstanding Technical Institution Award" by National Institute of Technical Teachers Training and Research, Chandigarh in the Northern region. CPC is the first Private Polytechnic College of Punjab to be honored by this prestigious award on 7th September '2017.

Located in the Vicinity of Chandigarh
